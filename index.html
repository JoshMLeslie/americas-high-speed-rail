The intention of this page is to document, with sufficient information to leverage in construction planning, a multi-country/continent high-speed rail system, connecting Canada, The USA, Mexico, and South America with the goal of enhancing socio-economic conditions across these regions and, in turn, globally.

While passenger rail is a major focus of HSR, for conventional rail, freight shipping overshadows it 4 to 1 [AAR, proper citation needed] and should be examined as the major source of investment for long distance HSR while passenger rail requirements are reserved for intercity/state transit. In other words, long distances for freight, short distances for passengers.
